How to Take Care of Your Flowers

Use clean water and vases Always use clean buckets or vases to avoid bacteria that can make flowers wilt quickly.

Remove extra leaves Remove leaves that are below the water line because they can rot and dirty the water.

Daily Flower Care Change the water every day Fresh water helps flowers last longer and keeps them smelling fresh.

Keep flowers in a cool place Avoid direct sunlight, electric fans, and too much heat because flowers dry out faster.

Mist the flowers lightly Spray a little water on petals to help keep them fresh and hydrated.

Check for wilted flowers Remove damaged or dead flowers immediately so the arrangement still looks beautiful.

For Flower Shop or Selling Display Store flowers properly If possible, place flowers in a refrigerated area or cool room during hot weather.

Handle flowers gently Avoid crushing petals or bending stems while arranging or delivering bouquets.

Use flower food Add flower food or a small amount of sugar and lemon to the water to help flowers last longer.

Extra Tip Good flower care makes customers happier because the bouquet stays fresh longer after they buy it. Fresh flowers also help your shop look more professional and attractive.
